The LeBron James Family Foundation

The LeBron James Family Foundation has invested its time, resources, and attention in the kids of James' hometown in Akron, Ohio for over a decade. Learn more about the foundations history and projects, including the I PROMISE School, here.

The I PROMISE Program

The I PROMISE Program helps students in James' hometown of Akron to succeed by providing them a chance at a fully paid for college education. You can find out more about how to qualify for the I PROMISE Scholarship and more here.
